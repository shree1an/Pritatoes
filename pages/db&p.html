Impact of technology on daily life and business & printing business

20th Jan, 2024

By Kabya,


The way people spend their lives has been profoundly altered by technology. People can now interact instantaneously and easily overcome distances because to the widespread use of cellphones, social media, and instant messaging, which has completely changed communication. Furthermore, a wealth of information is now easily accessible on the internet, including news, research, and educational materials. Business is the pillar of the economic growth of any country.The globalization process makes the world market as a village market by the use of modern technology. With the use of modern communication apps like What's app, Skype, and Zoom, multinational corporations may quickly connect with their clients. The global online market has grown to be a bustling economic hub in recent years. By putting startups on an even playing field with larger organisations, technology has completely changed the way businesses operate. In order to get a competitive edge in the market, small firms use a wide range of technology, including mobile devices and servers. Technology should be included into small business owners' planning processes to facilitate seamless integration and create space for future growth. Because of this, entrepreneurs can establish businesses utilising the best technology on the market. The impact of technology in business has flourished in many sectors of business. It is used in online software-based markets like Amazon, Ali Baba used technology for doing business in the global market.

A printer is a device that takes data from a computer, usually in the form of text and graphics, and prints it onto standard 8.5" by 11" sheets of paper. The size, speed, sophistication, and cost of printers varies. Printers are one of the common computer peripheral devices that can be classified into two categories that are 2D and 3D printers. The 2D printers are used to print text and graphics on a paper, and 3D printers are used to create three dimensional physical objects. Changes in technology have brought about an important change in the printing sector. The development of 3D printing has changed small-scale production and prototyping, making it possible to produce complex and customised goods. Web-to-print services are now widely available, allowing users to customise orders and submit print jobs online, which improves customer satisfaction and increases market share. Technology has enabled personalised printing based on individual tastes or data possible through variable data printing, which finds use in direct mail and marketing. Furthermore, the printing sector has embraced sustainability, using digital processes, eco-friendly inks, and sustainable methods to minimise their impact on the environment.
